Q14. / A14. This plot essentially graphs the fluctuations for a set of proteins which are listed and color coded on the right side of the plot. The black and colored lines are fairly similar in certain places but very different in others, particularly centered around residue 50 and ~140. The lines likely differ most in regions where the colored-line proteins’ flexibility is much more variable and/or higher than those represented by black lines.
